At Fells Prospect Community, we believe donors deserve complete transparency about how their contributions are used. Every dollar you give creates ripples of positive change throughout our community.

## Where Your Money Goes

We're proud of our financial efficiency:

- **92%** goes directly to programs and services
- **5%** covers administrative costs
- **3%** supports fundraising efforts

This breakdown exceeds industry best practices and reflects our commitment to maximizing your impact.

## Breaking Down a $100 Donation

Let's look at exactly what happens when you donate $100:

### Programs & Services ($92)
- **Education**: $35 - Tutoring materials, learning software, and curriculum development
- **Food Security**: $25 - Fresh produce, pantry staples, and meal preparation
- **Job Training**: $18 - Skills workshops, certification programs, and job placement
- **Healthcare**: $14 - Medical supplies, health screenings, and wellness programs

### Administrative ($5)
- Facility maintenance and utilities
- Technology infrastructure
- Financial management and auditing

### Fundraising ($3)
- Donor communications
- Event coordination
- Grant writing and reporting

## Real Impact Examples

### $25 Donation
- Provides school supplies for two students for a semester
- Funds 5 nutritious meals for families in need
- Covers materials for one job readiness workshop

### $100 Donation
- Supports one student in our tutoring program for a month
- Provides a week of groceries for a family of four
- Funds mental health counseling sessions for two individuals

### $500 Donation
- Sponsors a family's participation in our programs for three months
- Provides job training certification for one individual
- Funds a complete health screening for 25 community members

### $1,000+ Donation
- Sponsors an entire program cohort
- Funds equipment for job training programs
- Supports program expansion into new neighborhoods

## Our Commitment to Transparency

We maintain our commitment to transparency through:

1. **Annual Independent Audits**: Third-party financial reviews ensure accountability
2. **Quarterly Impact Reports**: Detailed metrics on how donations are used
3. **Open Financial Statements**: Published annually on our website
4. **GuideStar Platinum Seal**: Highest rating for nonprofit transparency
