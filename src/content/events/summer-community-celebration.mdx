## A Day to Remember!

Our 2024 Summer Community Celebration was a huge success! Over 2,000 community members joined us for a beautiful day of connection, celebration, and fun.

### Event Highlights

- **Food Trucks & Vendors**: 15 local food trucks and artisan vendors
- **Live Music**: Three stages featuring local bands and performers
- **Kids Zone**: Face painting, bounce houses, and games
- **Community Resource Fair**: 30+ local organizations and services
- **Talent Show**: Showcasing our community's incredible talents

## By the Numbers

- **2,000+** attendees
- **500** free meals served
- **200** kids participated in activities
- **30** community organizations represented
- **15** local food vendors
- **100+** volunteers made it possible
