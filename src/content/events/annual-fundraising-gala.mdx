## An Evening of Impact and Inspiration

Join us for our signature fundraising event of the year! The Annual Fundraising Gala brings together community leaders, supporters, and changemakers for an unforgettable evening supporting Hope Foundation's mission.

### Event Highlights

- **Welcome Reception** (6:00 PM): Cocktails and hors d'oeuvres
- **Dinner Service** (7:00 PM): Three-course gourmet meal
- **Impact Presentation** (8:00 PM): Hear directly from program participants
- **Live Auction** (8:30 PM): Exciting items and experiences
- **Dancing & Entertainment** (9:00 PM): Live band performance

## This Year's Theme: "Building Futures Together"

Our 2025 gala celebrates the incredible impact we've achieved together and looks forward to the bright futures we're helping to build in our community.
