## Join Us for a Day of Service!

Community Volunteer Day is one of our favorite events of the year! Join fellow community members for a day of meaningful service projects that directly benefit our programs and facilities.

### Projects Include

- **Garden Renovation**: Help plant vegetables for our community garden
- **Center Beautification**: Painting, cleaning, and organizing spaces
- **Food Bank Sorting**: Organize donations for upcoming food distributions
- **Reading Nook Setup**: Create a cozy reading space for our after-school program
- **Technology Setup**: Help configure computers in our new lab

No experience necessary - we'll provide all training and materials!

## Schedule

- **9:00 AM**: Volunteer check-in and coffee
- **9:30 AM**: Opening remarks and project assignments
- **10:00 AM - 12:30 PM**: Morning work session
- **12:30 PM - 1:30 PM**: Lunch (provided!)
- **1:30 PM - 3:00 PM**: Afternoon work session
- **3:00 PM**: Closing celebration and thank you

## What to Bring

- Comfortable work clothes (will get dirty!)
- Closed-toe shoes
- Water bottle
- Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
- Work gloves (if you have them)

We'll provide all tools, supplies, and refreshments.

## Perfect For

- Individuals looking to give back
- Families (children 8+ welcome with adult supervision)
- Corporate team building groups
- Student groups seeking service hours

## COVID-19 Protocols

We follow all local health guidelines. Projects will be conducted both indoors (with proper ventilation) and outdoors to ensure safety and comfort.

## Group Registration

Bringing a team of 5 or more? Email volunteer@hopefoundation.org for group coordination.

## Community Service Hours

Students and professionals requiring documentation of service hours will receive verification.
