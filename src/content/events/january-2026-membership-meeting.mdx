Join us for our January 2026 General Membership Meeting to kick off the new year!

## Event Details

Our monthly general membership meeting where we discuss community initiatives, neighborhood updates, and important matters affecting Fells Prospect.

**When:** Thursday, January 30th, 2026 at 7:00 PM
**Where:** Kisling's Tavern (Upstairs)

## What to Expect

- Community updates and announcements
- Discussion of ongoing initiatives
- Q&A with board members
- Networking with neighbors

## Meeting Schedule

General Membership Meetings are held on the **last Thursday of each month** at Kisling's Tavern (Upstairs) beginning at 7:00 PM, unless otherwise noted.

## Who Can Attend?

All Fells Prospect residents are welcome! You don't need to be a paying member to attend meetings, but we encourage you to join our community association to have voting rights on important community matters.
