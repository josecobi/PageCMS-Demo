## Build Your Financial Future

Join us for this free, four-part workshop series designed to help families and individuals build financial confidence and stability.

### Workshop Schedule

**Session 1: Budgeting Basics** (January 20)
- Creating a realistic household budget
- Tracking expenses effectively
- Identifying areas to save

**Session 2: Building Credit** (January 27)
- Understanding credit scores
- How to build and repair credit
- Managing credit cards responsibly

**Session 3: Saving Strategies** (February 3)
- Emergency fund essentials
- Setting and reaching savings goals
- Making your money work for you

**Session 4: Long-term Planning** (February 10)
- Retirement planning basics
- College savings options
- Insurance and protection strategies

## Who Should Attend

This workshop is perfect for:
- Anyone looking to improve their financial literacy
- Young adults starting their financial journey
- Families wanting to build financial security
- Individuals recovering from financial challenges

## What You'll Learn

- Practical budgeting techniques
- Credit score improvement strategies
- Effective saving methods
- Basic investment concepts
- Resources for continued learning

- **Expert Instructors**: Certified financial educators
- **Interactive Learning**: Hands-on exercises and discussions
- **Free Workbook**: Take-home materials and resources
