David brings creativity and strategic thinking to Hope Foundation's development efforts. Since joining in 2019, he has increased annual fundraising by 60% and expanded our donor base significantly. His background in corporate partnerships and major gifts has helped secure critical funding for program expansion.

Prior to Hope Foundation, David worked in development for a large healthcare nonprofit and as a marketing executive in the private sector. He holds a BA in Communications and a certificate in Nonprofit Management.

David is known for his ability to tell compelling stories that connect donors with the impact of their generosity. He's also an accomplished photographer who documents our programs and events.
