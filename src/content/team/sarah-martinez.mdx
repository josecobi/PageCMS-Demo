With a Master's in Public Health and 15 years of experience in program management, Sarah has been instrumental in expanding Fells Prospect Community's reach and impact. She joined our team in 2017 and has since launched five new programs, including our mental health initiative and job training program.

Sarah's collaborative leadership style brings together staff, volunteers, and community partners to create programs that truly meet community needs. She's particularly passionate about using data to drive decision-making and continuous improvement.

A lifelong advocate for health equity, Sarah previously worked with public health departments and community clinics before joining Fells Prospect Community.
