A respected business leader and community advocate, Robert has served on Hope Foundation's Board since 2012 and as Chair since 2018. His background in corporate finance and strategic planning has been invaluable in guiding the organization through periods of significant growth.

Robert is the retired CFO of a Fortune 500 company and serves on several nonprofit and corporate boards. He's particularly passionate about economic mobility and believes in Hope Foundation's holistic approach to community development.

Under his board leadership, Hope Foundation has expanded from three to five community centers and increased our endowment by 200%.
