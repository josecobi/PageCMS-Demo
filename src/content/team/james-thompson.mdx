Dr. James Thompson has dedicated his career to building stronger communities through education, economic development, and social services. Before joining Fells Prospect Community, he served as Program Director for a national community development organization and taught nonprofit management at State University.

He holds a PhD in Public Administration and Master's degrees in Social Work and Business Administration. Under his leadership, Fells Prospect Community has grown from serving 5,000 community members annually to over 15,000, while maintaining our commitment to quality and sustainability.

When not working, Dr. Thompson volunteers as a mentor for aspiring nonprofit leaders and serves on the boards of several community organizations.
