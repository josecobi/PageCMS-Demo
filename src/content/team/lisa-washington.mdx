Lisa's passion for community service is contagious. Since joining Hope Foundation in 2020, she has transformed our volunteer program, creating structured training, recognition programs, and meaningful opportunities for community members to get involved.

Under her leadership, volunteer satisfaction scores have reached 95%, and volunteer retention has increased significantly. She has a special gift for matching volunteers with roles where they can truly shine.

Lisa previously managed volunteer programs for a food bank and animal shelter. She holds a BA in Sociology and believes that volunteering builds stronger, more connected communities.
