A former elementary school teacher with a Master's in Education, Jennifer joined Fells Prospect Community in 2018 to create greater impact beyond the traditional classroom. She has developed curriculum for our tutoring programs, launched our college prep initiative, and built partnerships with local schools.

Jennifer's programs serve over 3,500 students annually, with impressive results: 85% of students improve their grades, and 95% of high school seniors go on to college or vocational training.

She's passionate about educational equity and believes every child deserves access to quality learning opportunities regardless of their circumstances.
