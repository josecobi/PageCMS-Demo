With expertise in nonprofit operations and facilities management, Michael keeps Hope Foundation's five community centers running at peak performance. He oversees everything from building maintenance to IT infrastructure, ensuring staff and program participants have the resources they need.

Michael joined our team in 2016 after working in facilities management for a large nonprofit health system. His proactive approach to maintenance and his talent for finding cost-effective solutions have saved the organization thousands of dollars while improving service delivery.

He holds a degree in Business Administration and certifications in facilities management and nonprofit operations.
